## Problem Statement
    People need to know the weather so they can plan thier day according to it. So we need a app to give a rough idea of weather of today and next few days. An app to help people to have a prefect plan for  their days

## Scope of this project
    this program will show 
    1) the current weather of the city
    2) timing of sunset and sunrise
    3) 5 days forecast
    4)graphical representation of 5 days minimum and maximum temperture 

## Target users
    1)normal people who wants to know about the weather
    2)travellers who have to travel 
    3)people who are planning for outting
    4)farmers to see the weather

## High-level features
    1)give weather of any city 
    2)gives next 5 days forecast
    3)simple and easy coding
